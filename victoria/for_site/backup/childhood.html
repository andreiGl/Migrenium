ABOVE: Byelorussian magazine "Slujzba bytu Belarusi" (Feb, 1986) published the article about successful participants of the Republican Competition and their honorable works.

She started in Belarus, Russia...

Victoria was born in Minsk, capital of Belarus.
 
1980-1984
Technological College, Minsk, Belarus.
Specialization: Hairstyling Art and Decorative Make-Up
Diploma: Artist - Designer
 
1986-1988
Graduate School of Design, Lvov, Ukraine.
Diploma: Designer Hairstylist
 
Awards:
 
October 1985: City of Minsk Competition of Hairdressers and Cosmetologists, participant.
 

She won not just once...

December 1985: Republic of Belarus Competition of Hairdressers and Cosmetologists, First Place among Junior Competitors under 21 years of age. Promoted to Hairdresser of the First Class, Honorable Diploma.
 
LEFT: Victoria has won the 1st place. She is a third from the right on this picture.
RIGHT: Model Ilona shows the winning style to the judges.
...because her hands are magic...

July 1986: All Union Competition of Hairdressers and Cosmetologists, awarded Honorable Diploma of "Golden Hands Award" and as a Promotion has been sent to study at the All Union Designers Courses for Hairstylists of High Qualification.
 
October 1989: City of Minsk Competition of Hairdressers and Cosmetologists, First Place, Honorable Diploma.
