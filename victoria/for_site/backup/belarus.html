ABOVE: Byelorussian magazine "Slujzba bytu Belarusi" (Jun, 1989) published the article about Victoria's working in Minsk hairdressing salon.

After successful completion of Graduate School of Design Victoria came back to Belarus to continue her rising career as a Designer-Hairstylist. Her creative work had again captured attention not just her regular clients who returned to her after two years of absence but the media's attention too.
 
Victoria had done an interview for the Byelorussian Radio followed by an article in the magazine "Slujzba Bytu Belarusi", and another one in the popular newspaper.
 
Her popularity soared beyond her imagination as she became the youngest Hairstylist-Designer in Minsk - city of two million.
 
She participated in numerous competitions and seminars in St. Petersburg, Kiev, Riga, Minsk.
 
Her creative work also includes:
 
* Teaching classes at the Technological College;
* Volunteering at the City Experimental Lab for Design;
* Judging of Competitions.

It's amazing work, Victoria!
