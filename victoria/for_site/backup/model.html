ABOVE, RIGHT and BELOW: Victoria as a model.
She can do a lot...

Believe it or not, but Victoria had started her career as a model. At 16 she was the one selected among tens of thousands competitors - young ladies - for the runway shows in the major "House of Fashion". After working for two years as a runway model part time and attending college full time she decided to stop her modeling carrier and start the new one as a hairstylist.
 
In the beginning she combined her two interests and use to model for successful hairdressers and make-up artists for various competitions.


As she's gained more experience as a hairdresser she started competing herself while selecting her own models, some of which were recommended and especially chosen for her by the team of teachers at the same college that she finished earlier.

...and whatever she does,.. she does perfectly
