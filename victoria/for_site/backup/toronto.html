She came here a long ago...

Who doesn't know that beginnings are tough? How about starting in a foreign country with very little money ( $333 ), nowhere to live, with no English what so ever, no job, no acquaintances, but a HUGE desire to succeed?


ABOVE: This is Yonge Street. On the right from Victoria the Loblaw's store has risen by now.

ABOVE: Victoria with her cousin Julia and brother Alex on the eve of Julia's wedding. Julia's style done by Victoria.
She started from the beginning...

It's not only did she go to ESL classes to learn the basics of the language, but also took courses at Seneca College to make her accent go away as much as possible. Are you surprised? She can share her secret with you...
 
Actually, there's no secret, just this feeling that everything will be OK in a while. It's true, because Canada is the perfect country for success. Try your best, and your dreams will come true!

...to be a winner again!

Victoria came to Canada in February 1990.
 
Working way:
 
1990-1992: "The "Cut Above", Hairstylist, 5623 Yonge Street
 
1993-2001: "Victoria's Hair Design" at "Strike a Pose", Self Employed, 5623 Yonge Street
 
2001-present: "Victoria's Hair Design" at "Public Image", Self Employed, 5291 Yonge Street


What is Victoria now?
 
Now Victoria's happy with renting a working place in "Public Image" and having a lot of clients who sincerely love her and her fantastic work.
 
She is married, lives in a lovely condo (not far from her work). She likes traveling with her husband all around the world. Their favorite places to visit are Italy, France, Czech Republic, and Europe in whole. Victoria adores skiing, skating, gymnastics. And she has a huge painting picture on her home wall to finish.
